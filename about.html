About Me

My name is Anand Pandya. I was born in India and graduated from Gujarat University with a bachelor in commerce. I have always had a huge interest in music since a young age and I started out Akash sound as a small business in India. After coming to Canada in 2008, I have found huge success and have been fortunate enough to work with many huge names in this industry. I have performed in many events over the years and have been steadily improving with each one. I am proud of my success so far and hope to continue this legacy. I also have interest in electronics and fixing things is a big hobby of mine, please feel free to use the contact us option on this website if you have any questions or simply if you wish to connect!
